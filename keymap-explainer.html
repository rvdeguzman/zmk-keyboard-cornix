Special behaviors
  • Esc / Ctrl mod-tap (mt_esc): tap for Esc, hold for Ctrl. Hold-preferred flavor, 150 ms tapping term — a quick tap sends Esc; anything held past 150 ms (or interrupted by another key) acts as Ctrl.
  • Adjust is conditional: it activates automatically whenever Lower and Raise are both held — there is no dedicated Adjust key.
  • Encoders (all layers): left knob = Volume up/down, right knob = Pause-Break / Scroll Lock. Pressing either knob = Mute.
  • Bluetooth (Adjust layer): BT 0/1/2 select paired profiles; BT Clr forgets the current profile.
